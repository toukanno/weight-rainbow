## Description
Rainbow Weight Log is a local-first weight tracker designed for simple daily use.

Record weight manually, by voice, or by loading a scale photo. The app shows BMI and trend history as wellness reference information, with clear non-medical messaging.

Only name, height, weight, age, and gender are handled in the app. Records are stored locally on the device and are not uploaded to external servers by the current build.

The app is planned as a free release. The current build includes only a placeholder for future ad placement and does not include an ad SDK or tracking.
